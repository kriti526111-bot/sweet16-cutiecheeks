Letter 01: The Person You Are

It will make no laugh but our first ever meet wasn't really a good one. I thought you were this snobbish girl who was way too proud of herself. *heh yea*

Few days later, I finally got to put down my perception about you. Before this, did I wrong you, but trust me, you were so fun to hang out with. You made me feel so comfortable around you.

After day 1, it happened. Some things happened and you cried. But seeing you cry made me realize how pure your soul is and how pure your thoughts are. It was so easy to make you smile again.

Letter 02: The Beginning

You are seen the kind of pain that makes you strong and brave. You've been through things that would break most people.

And something has taught you that you're tougher than you think. You've learned that even when things seem impossible, you can still find a way to keep going. You've learned to be brave even when you're scared. The comforting thing is, you're strong enough to face whatever comes next. And that's something to be really proud of.

You are so much understanding, light hearted and fun to be around. I'm sure you'll stay the same quirky goofy person you are.

Letter 03: The Memories

This somehow was supposed to be about our memories but let me be honest, we don't really have much to talk about.

There have been so many exchanges of words between us but those words have been more of us complaining about things, ranting and laughing. Those words have been more of us fighting over things and us not talking to each other for days.

But I'm glad we always found our way back. We've been through a lot and I'm really grateful for having you in my life.

Letter 04: The Impact

I know the biggest impact you've had on me is teaching me to not care about what others think.

It's not like I wasn't doing before you came, but it really made me realize how important it is to just be yourself.

Whenever something happens, good or bad, you're always there. You never judge me for anything. You're always so kind and understanding.

Letter 05: Things I Never Say Enough

There are a lot of things I don't say enough, and this is probably one of them.

Thank you for everything. Sometimes I think about my important people and I realize that I got the best ones. They're usually blessed because I feel like I'm the best thing that happened to them but I'm grateful for having you in my life.

I just want you to know that you mean a lot to me and I'm so glad we met.

Letter 06: The Future

To write about the future, it feels a little scary because I don't really know what the future holds for us.

But I do know that I want you in it. I want to see you grow and achieve all that you've dreamed of. Not biggest, not smallest, just whatever makes you happy and content.

That's all I've more birthdays to gift. So I'll stop here.
